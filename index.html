My Favorite Hobby is Origami!

My favorite hobby is origami because it's very fun to see the final product after you've sent some of your time on it. Even if it takes a couple minutes, or it takes a month, it's quite nice. Also, you don't need to bring special pieces of paper sometimes; you can just use a Post-It or a piece of paper after cutting it into a square. Overall, it's very easy to learn as well.
